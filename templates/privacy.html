Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a MoneyGoes account and use the Service, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided during registration.
  • Expense data — transaction amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and features used, to help us improve the Service.

We do not collect payment card details or any financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used solely to operate and improve MoneyGoes. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain your personal expense tracking dashboard.
  • Authenticate your identity and keep your account secure.
  • Respond to support requests or enquiries you submit.
  • Analyse aggregated, anonymised usage patterns to improve app features.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, and regular security reviews.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us through the app. Upon a valid deletion request, your personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

MoneyGoes may use a limited number of trusted third-party services to support core functionality — for example, hosting infrastructure or error monitoring. These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data only as necessary to perform services on our behalf and may not use it for their own purposes.

We do not integrate with third-party advertising networks, analytics resellers, or social media tracking pixels.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the way we handle your data, please reach out to us through the in-app contact form. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.

This policy may be updated periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email.
